Psalm 50.11

A prayer

Oh dear Lord, what are you going to do to me? What are you going to do with me? What is the lot for the "gay christian?" And worse, what is the lot for said man who thinks he's some kind of animal? Your churchgoes say it, there is no place for me here. I am diseased and need a hospital wherein I will be cured.

My love is likened to cancer. It's held to the same pity as alcoholism. That I must have been abused, or molested, or that I've been decieved, or possessed.

Dear Lord, your churchgoers delight in my misery. They make me an object of ridicule. They wait to see me suffer. They want to see me dead. They watch eagerly to see your righteous wrath poured out on me. Lord, I hope that when you do, that at least they'll be horrified, and not proud.

Dear Lord, if ever I have doubted that you still love me, your churchgoers will gladly remind me that you don't.

Forgive me, Lord, for being a pathogen to your communion; a poison to your body.

Forgive your churchgoers, because they might mean well, and only want to scare me away from the torments you've prepared for me.

Oh Saints with gentle old faces, oh Mary, mother of God, shed a tear for me while you say Thy will be done.

Oh Body of Christ, who touched my own defiled body, like a diseased leper, I can only thank you that you didn't destroy me that instant.

Sorry to the old churchgoers when they found out that their sweetest boy was a revolting, abominable freak.

Sorry to the musicians who were scandalized when they found out that their worship was being infiltrated by an unclean drummer. They must have felt such pain that they never met his eyes again.

Sorry to those churchgoers who didn't know, and wondered why their beloved scripture reader suddenly couldn't read anymore.

Sorry to the priest who is dismayed that my love is no longer punishable by jail time in this country. He must have been nostalgic.

And sorry to the reader for calling my love my love. I should know better, that it's not love at all, and that my everlasting suffering to come is true love, fulfilled. Forgive me for misleading you.

Dear Lord, I cannot hate you, and I don't want to. Lord, if you are my father, and if I'm your son, please love me. Here on earth fathers hate their sons. I can only ask that you are greater than them.
